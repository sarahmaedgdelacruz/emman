🍝 My Favorite Foods 🍳

When life gets tough, Emman turns to his comfort trio — sisig, coffee, and carbonara. The sizzling sound of sisig reminds him that even the hottest moments can be turned into something flavorful. A cup of coffee keeps his spirit awake, fueling his dreams and ideas. And of course, carbonara — creamy and comforting — reminds him that life, like pasta, is best enjoyed with a touch of warmth and joy.

Carbonara

Perfectly creamy and savory — a taste of comfort!

Coffee

Rich, aromatic, and energizing — the perfect companion!

Sisig

A sizzling dish of chopped meat, onions, and spices — a true Filipino classic!
